Welcome to Meals & Miles

Meals & Miles is a web application designed to connect food and travel enthusiasts. Users can share their meal experiences and travel stories, creating a vibrant community of like-minded individuals. With features such as user registration, login, and profile management, Meals & Miles provides a platform for users to express their culinary and travel adventures. Whether you're a food lover or a travel enthusiast, Meals & Miles is the perfect place to share your experiences and connect with others who share your passions.
